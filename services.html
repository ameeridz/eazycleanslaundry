How Does it Work?

Washing

We conveniently offer full-service laundry, dry cleaning, and pickup services all in one place, eliminating the need for multiple trips to different venues.

Rinsing

Rinse the clothes by pulling them in and out of the water for two to three minutes. This will help to remove any soap still on them. Check that the clothes look clean, not soapy. If there is still soap on them, dump out the water in the tub and rinse them again with clean water.

Drying

Our facilities offer a large number of modern machines that will have your clothes perfectly clean and dried within an hour. We can also pick up your laundry and return it to you within 24 hours with our delivery service.
